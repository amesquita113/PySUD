Here is a brief description of the stats used in PySUD:

Strength:	Strength is your main damage dealing stat for Fighters.  The
		more strenght you have, the harder you hit.
		
Dexterity:	Your reflexes, balance and hand/eye coordination.  Good stat
		for light armor wearers to increase their Armor Class.  Used
		by all classes for initiative rolls.

Constitution:	Your characters health and stamina.  More constitution means
		a sturdy character with higher health.

Wisdom:		Wisdom is the main damage dealing stat for Sorcerers.  The
		more wisdom you have, the more damage your spells do.
