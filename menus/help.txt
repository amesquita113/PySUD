Command help:

	LOOK  - Looks around the room
	LEVEL - Shows how much XP needed to level
	LIST  - Lists the items for sale in a shop
	BUY   - Buys an item when in a shop
	SELL  - Sells an item when in a shop
	STATS - Lists your current stats
	CAMP  - Setup camp and logs you out of the game
	REST  - Rests to recover health (1 health/second)
	EQUIP - Equips a weapon or armor
	MAP   - Displays a zone map
	INVENTORY - Lists what you have on your person

	N - Moves north
	S - Moves south
	E - Moves east
	W - Moves west
